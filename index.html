My main focus is solving real-world AI system problems — I move across product, engineering, and open-ended research depending on what a problem needs. One part of that is trustworthy AI: whether evaluations measure what they claim, and whether safeguards survive contact with adversaries. Current threads:

  • Recommender systems — retrieval & representation behind large-scale feeds.
