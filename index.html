About

I build measurement infrastructure for AI systems and study where it breaks. Current threads: stress-testing LLM-based safety monitors under distribution shift, auditing the benchmarks used to certify model behavior, and the retrieval and representation systems behind large-scale recommender feeds.

I'm pursuing an MS in Computer Science at Georgia Tech (2025–2027) and spending the summer as an ML engineer intern on LinkedIn's feed recommendation team (Feed AI). Before that I worked with the NVIDIA/GT Efficient & Intelligent Computing Lab on LLM evaluation and agentic AI, and earlier on graph learning and causal analysis for biomedical data at Emory. The through-line is evaluation — I care about whether the numbers we report mean what we claim they mean.
